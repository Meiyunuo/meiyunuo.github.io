Rabbit

Installation. Material: nail tips, tree, hair, fingernail, human frame model

Size: 210 Ã— 160 cm

Time: 2022

Description: I embedded the nail tips in the tree, wrapped my hair around it and kept it in place. Amid the myriad pieces, I concealed a fragment of my actual nail. The inverted tree is supported by human ribs, and the entire installation resembles the outstretched claws of a giant eagle as it hunts its prey, while audiences are now facing the rabbit. My attempt to graft a part of the human body to a tree stems from my imagination of the hybridization of life. It reflects an unrestrained contemplation of the distinctive landscape in today's pluralistic and post-human era characterized by hybridization. It's like a Sword of Damocles hanging over our heads threatening us at any moment, and you and I will end up shivering like rabbits under the eagle's claws.
