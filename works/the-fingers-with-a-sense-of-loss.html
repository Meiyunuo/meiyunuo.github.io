The Fingers with a Sense of Loss

Type: Images

Material: Wooden blinds

Size: 150 × 135 cm

Time: 2025

Description: It is both furniture and ruin. This is a collapse of structure—like abandoned skin, it has lost the surface to cling to. Like a limb with loosened joints, it hangs slack, as if caught in the midst of a transformation into something unknowable. Weary and unresolved, it falters in the process of becoming, struggling to settle into a new form.
