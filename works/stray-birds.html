Stray Birds

Installation

Material: nail tips, tree, knife

Size: 183 × 40 × 95 cm

Time: 2021

作品阐释：Nail tips are stuck to the metal kitchen knives to resemble wings and then scattered randomly on the branch. We can see trees, knives and nail tips, but we cannot see birds, because stray birds are an imagination. The knife's sharpness and the nail tips' softness create a set of contradictory and unrelated images, as in Tagore's poem: "There are no traces of wings in the sky, but I have flown."
