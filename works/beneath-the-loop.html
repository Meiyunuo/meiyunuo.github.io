Beneath the Loop

Type: Video

Duration: 1 min 2 sec

Time: 2025

Description: Beneath the Loop traces how the body is gently pulled apart by time within a given space. In the empty circular pool, the performer repeatedly walks and gathers fallen leaves; these aimless gestures make the body seem held by the space, yet softly estranged from it. Gradually, the body loses a sense of autonomy, as if guided by the environment or an unnamed force. The boundary between self and world begins to blur. Through this moving-image approach, viewers can sense the body’s after-images—its echoes within the space—and the subtle, spectral presence that appears as perception loosens and edges dissolve.
